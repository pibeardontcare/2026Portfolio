Keeping the Lights On

Honoring the infrastructure teams who kept the world connected when it needed to be most.

Client

Facebook Engineering

Type

Brand Campaign

Context

COVID-19 Pandemic

During the pandemic, the invisible became essential. Facebook's infrastructure teams — the engineers and technicians keeping servers running, networks stable, and communities connected — were doing some of the most important work in the world without recognition.

Keeping the Lights On was a campaign built to change that. It connected communities to the people behind the infrastructure they depended on — giving faces, names, and stories to the teams that made remote work, remote school, and staying in touch with family possible.

A project about the profound human impact of deeply technical work — and a reminder that infrastructure isn't abstract. It's the reason someone got to see their grandmother on her birthday.
