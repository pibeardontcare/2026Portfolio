Keeping the Lights On

Honoring the infrastructure teams who kept the world connected when it needed to be most.

Client

Facebook Engineering

Type

Brand Campaign

Context

COVID-19 Pandemic

When the world paused, connection became infrastructure. During the pandemic, Facebook's engineering teams quietly held communities together — keeping networks stable and services running when millions of people depended on them for healthcare information, family contact, and basic survival.

This campaign honored that work. We connected people to the individuals behind the infrastructure they depended on — the engineers who kept food bank logistics running, enabled virtual elder care visits, helped small businesses stay visible during lockdowns, and kept churches able to livestream services to congregations who couldn't gather.

A project about the profound human weight of deeply technical work. Infrastructure isn't abstract — it's the reason someone got to see their grandmother on her birthday, or found emergency housing in a crisis. Keeping the Lights On gave that work the recognition it deserved.
