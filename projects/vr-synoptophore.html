VR Synoptophore

Immersive diagnostic tool for binocular vision assessment — accurate to within 5.4 prism diopters.

Domain

Medical / Clinical

Platform

VR Headset

Accuracy

±5.4 Prism Diopters

A synoptophore is a large, expensive piece of clinical equipment used by ophthalmologists to assess and treat conditions like strabismus (misaligned eyes). It requires a dedicated clinical setting and trained staff — creating significant access barriers worldwide.

This project replaced the analog device entirely with a VR headset experience designed to perform the same diagnostic function. Clinical testing validated the approach, showing measurement accuracy within 5.4 prism diopters — meeting clinical thresholds for meaningful assessment.

The implications extend beyond convenience: a portable VR diagnostic means rural clinics, telehealth programs, and under-resourced settings could access tools previously limited to well-funded hospitals.
